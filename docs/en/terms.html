2.The service

Tide It provides, for information purposes, tide, weather and marine conditions for ports and spots worldwide:

  • tide times, heights and coefficients computed by harmonic analysis from constants published by official hydrographic offices;
  • weather conditions (Apple Weather / WeatherKit) and marine conditions;
  • real-time wind measured by third-party station networks (see section 5);
  • outing windows ("GO") and activity ratings computed against your own criteria, purely indicative.

  • The subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24h before the end of the current period; renewal is charged within the 24h preceding expiry.

  • Free month: on first install, Premium access is offered for 1 month, with no commitment and no payment method. When this period ends, the app automatically returns to the free tier unless you voluntarily subscribe. This offer applies once and does not affect existing subscribers.
